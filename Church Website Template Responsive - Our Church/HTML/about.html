New Leaf Church DNA

New Leaf is an interactive New Testament style house church that by nature focuses on relationships and personal change. With Social distancing, this is an unusual time for the church, but we continue to work to serve our community.

NLC doesn't "gather for church" as much as to come together as a family to learn God's word, support each other, and go out into the community to share with others. We try to balance gathering for worship by going out to serve.

As a house church, NLC is less about supporting property and structure. Instead, we can be free to be more focused on relationships and ministry in the community. Even with the lock-down, we were able to get out into the community this past month, doing community Prayer Walking and participated in Little Elm's Clean and Green program. These were planned as ways to support the needs of people and to give back to the community.


Do You Want More From Life?

If you would like something more than the traditional church, a desire to grow in your relationship with God and with your neighbors in the area or just would like to be a part of something trying to reach out to make a difference in our community, come to check out New Leaf Church.

Another Heading

NLC has always met of Saturday evenings in Little Elm, Texas -- not for theological reasons, but rather simply hoping to provide worship options for those who can't do Sunday morning. There have always been Sunday morning worship options but Saturday night worship isn't always so readily available.

I have grown to love Saturday worship as a housechurch. It is such a family-friendly, relational venue. And isn't that what the church should be?
